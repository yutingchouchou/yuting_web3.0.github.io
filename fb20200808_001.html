2020-08-08 18:42｜TFT 粉專

＼

【緣起】

加入「為台灣而教」的組織，使我的身分從一名大學生、助教、研究生，突然地轉變為小學老師，聽聞此訊，一位年過八旬的老教授與我娓娓道來，40年前，他教過一位優秀的物理系女學生，畢業不久，便自發地跑到蘭嶼教書，一年後，不知道怎地歸來做物理，除了那一年得練習與小學生打交道之外，回來後做物理也更顯吃力。

保守又沉穩的老教授給我說了這麼一個故事，並無就此打住，後續的補充，那位女學生後來也在物理之路上攻讀了博士、赴美做了博士後研究，最終…成家立業帶孩子，發現自己最大的興趣是在帶孩子。　

那我呢?物理和教育，我兩條路可以兼顧且堅固嗎?

且給我兩年的時間，試著實踐一些事情吧!

物理領域最著名的科普期刊，

叫做「今日物理」(Physics Today)

為表我在偏鄉教書仍不忘本的精神，

我乃取名為「物理二日」(Physics two days)

意義為一到五的戮力教書，六日兩天的物理思考與研究。

目前的規劃:

六:反省分享一週的教學心得體悟。

日:反省分享一週的教學心得體悟，讓我想起的物理。

【Origin】

Becoming a training teacher in TFT(Teach For Taiwan) changed my identity as a undergraduate student、teaching assistant and master student into an elementary school teacher suddenly.

Hearing about this information, an 80-year-old professor told me a story smoothly:

40 years ago, there was a girl in physics department, and she was one of his best students. After graduating from NTU, she taught voluntarily (spontaneously) in a small island near Taiwan called Lanyu. But somehow she quit teaching one year later and returned to the physics field. A year without practice had made her rusty in physical research. She continued studying PhD and worked as a post doctor in America. However, she found her biggest dream is to raise children after having a family.

What about me? Can I consolidate both education and physics at the same time (simultaneously)?

Giving me two years, and let’s see what I can achieve!

“Physics today” is the most famous popular science journal in the physics field.

To represent my steadfast mind of studying physics, I name it

”Physics two days”

Work hard teaching for elementary kids during weekdays, but study and think about physics on the two day holiday.

Current plan:

Saturday: Reflecting and sharing my teaching during the week.

Sunday: what inspires me when reflecting and sharing my teaching.
