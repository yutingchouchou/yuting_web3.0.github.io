【返校】

今日返校觀察到一個數據，如下圖，即便總人數很少是預期內的，但男女比的失衡，似乎無法用人數太少的誤差一筆帶過。也曾耳聞同一地區其它偏鄉學校，十年來有比例失衡的現象(男生比例增高)，大家有些什麼看法呢?　

我突然想起兩種概念：

1.

男女最本質的差異，是染色體。

男XY

女XX

也就是女性只會產生X的卵子，決定性別的任務自然會落在男性精子是X或Y上。古早有種說法，Y比X輕許多(如圖)，導致游速較快Y以更高的受精機率!　

2.

女性的XX染色體，其中一個X會在母體環境中，去活性化(被環境因子鎖住，沒基因表現)，只有其中一個X會表現。

精子的X在母體環境中，是否有可能被干擾而使受精機率下降呢?

以上兩種狀況，是否有在這幾年，更容易發生呢?

【Back To School】

I have noticed a phenomenon when returning school to clean the environment ravaged by typhoon. Even though few students is expected, it seems hard to explain the unbalanced gender ratio by error bar. I have heard the ratio has been gradually out of balance in the past ten years.(Proportion of boy is larger and larger.).

What’s your viewpoint about this phenomenon?

Two concepts struck me:

1.

The most fundamental difference between genders in science is chromosome.

Male:XY

Female:XX

In other word, female can only produce a X egg, so sex is determined by whether the sperm produced by male is X or Y.

Some rumors told us sperm Y is lighter than sperm X makes sperm Y swim faster than sperm X then to achieve successful fertilization.

2.

One of the X chromosomes in female’s XX would be inactivated randomly in female’s body (XCI). On the other hand, the inactivated X chromosome is locked by some chemical material and its gene cannot work.

Is it possible for sperm X being inactivated when swimming in female’s body? 　

Are above two conditions easily happening in this day?
