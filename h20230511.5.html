【泰戈爾詩集選】

今日乃泰戈爾系列演講的最終場，由印度駐台大使Amb. Gourangalal Das(戴國瀾)主講，現場備有印度傳統點心Ladoo！

然而，已經連續參與三場、問了兩個問題、自認參與誠意滿滿的我，拿了點心便烙跑了。

本欲為明天一場重要的Meeting做準備，殊不知何方的力量，竟然拽著我，即使不在會場聽講，仍然一首首地看著泰戈爾的詩句，細細地品嘗著Ladoo與一百首詩句，揀選出了15首與大家分享：

1.

We read the world wrong and say that it deceives us.

2.

I cannot choose the best, the best chooses me.

3.

the stars are not afraid to appear like fireflies.

4.

Don‘t cry because it is over, smile because it happened.

5.

The dust receives insult and in return offers her flowers.

6.

Set the bird’s wings with gold and it will never again soar in the sky .

7.

By plucking her petals you do not gather the beauty of the flower.

8.

If you shed tears when you miss the sun, you also miss the stars.

9.

Beauty, find thyself in love, not in the flattery of thy mirror.

10

If you shut your door to all errors, truth will be shut out.

11.

Only through the hellish hone, can have the power to create heaven.

Only through the Bleeding fingers,can play the tune of the world.

12.

To the world you may be one person, but to one person you may be the world.

13.

My heart, the bird of the wilderness, has found its sky in your eyes.

14.

Never frown, even when you are sad, because you never know who is falling in love with your smile.

15.

The sparrow is sorry for the peacock at the burden of its tail.

＃中文翻譯？？

當時負責幫泰戈爾翻譯的徐志摩有這麼一段小故事：

「詩人氣質和個性還使志摩經常意氣用事。如泰戈爾在杭州演出後，聽眾滿懷期待等著他翻譯，但他慢地立起來，很莊嚴地對台下聽眾說：

“他講的話，一句一句都是詩。詩是不能翻譯的，翻譯就是陰霾。你們已經看到泰戈爾先生的風度，已經聽到泰戈爾先生的聲音，你們有福了。聽呀·他在哪裡是在說話，簡單直接是夜鶯在唱歌——這是一種天籟。你們再聽他唱梵歌，我是不能翻譯的。”」

志摩啊志摩！真詩人。讓我聯想到霸王別姬-程蝶衣對虞姬的堅持。

詩癡與戲癡，不風魔，不成活！
